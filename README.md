## 🔍 Project Overview  

The objective of this project is to analyze the movie industry by exploring:

- Revenue dynamics over time  
- Budget vs revenue relationships  
- Genre performance and audience preferences  
- Cast & crew impact on box office success  

The project follows a complete end-to-end pipeline:

1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)**
2. **Data Analysis & Querying (SQL)**
3. **Data Visualization & Storytelling (Power BI)**  

---

## 🐍 Data Cleaning (Python)  

Python was used to preprocess and standardize the dataset before analysis.

### Key steps:
- Data inspection (`.info()`, `.describe()`, `.head()`)
- Handling missing values and inconsistencies  
- Removing duplicates  
- String cleaning (whitespace removal across object columns)  
- Data type corrections  
- Feature preparation for analysis  

This phase ensures **data quality, consistency, and reliability**, which is essential for accurate analysis.

---

## 🗄️ Data Analysis (SQL)  

SQL was used to extract business insights and answer key analytical questions.

### Key analyses performed:
- Aggregations of revenue, budget, and ratings  
- Identification of top-performing movies and genres  
- Revenue distribution analysis  
- Comparison between high-budget and low-budget movies  
- Subqueries for advanced filtering (e.g. above-average performance)  
- GROUP BY + HAVING for business-driven conditions  

### Example use cases:
- Which movies generate above-average revenue?  
- Which genres contribute the most to total revenue?  
- How does budget impact revenue performance?  
- What is the distribution of revenue across different categories?  

This step transforms raw data into **structured business insights** ready for decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard (Power BI)  

The cleaned and structured data was used to build an interactive dashboard focused on:

- Executive overview of industry performance  
- Movie-level performance analysis  
- Genre and audience insights  
- Cast & crew impact analysis  

The dashboard is designed with a strong focus on **business storytelling and usability**.

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

- Revenue is highly concentrated in a small number of blockbuster movies  
- High-budget films tend to generate higher revenue, but with high variability  
- Adventure and Action genres dominate the market  
- Ratings and revenue are not always aligned  
- A small group of actors and directors significantly influences box office results  

---

## 🚀 Business Impact  

This analysis supports:

- Strategic investment decisions in high-performing genres  
- Budget optimization strategies  
- Risk assessment in movie production  
- Identification of high-value actors and directors  
- Better understanding of market concentration
